Who I met

Josh Hudgins - Google Product Manager Cassandra Cummings - Director of Content, Product School

What I learned

I learned that one of the most important traits that a good product manager can have is understanding the problem. The speaker made a point that stuck with me which is that it is better to have the right problem rather than the right solution. He gave several examples of where data can mislead people if they don't use their intuition/heurisitc to understand it appropriately. He also made clear that the product manager's role is to maintain the vision and ensure that all of the potential blockers to executing that vision are removed.
